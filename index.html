Our Business

We understand how hard it is to startup a business while having a fulltime job. Having a minimum viable product while being fully commited to a great employer takes up too much risk to make the transition without having a previous market validation.

Prospecting the desired industry and companies is time consuming. It also requires specific skills in sales approach and business knowledge for the product to succeed in the market.

Competition is everywhere, so in order to stand out it is necessary an eagle eye for customer selection and superb sales mixin.

We often work with talented software engineers and marketing gurus, with unique minimum viable product ready for growth and scalability.

We start by evaluating the actual status of the product related to the short and mid term desired sales goals.
