About Us

We are a sales consultant company that helps selected software engineers, developers, lead designers and marketing leaders take their minimum viable product scale into a corporate level businesses.

Who we are

We have over ten years of experience guiding startup companies on finding their path to success. We provide support on analyzing the product strengths, finding the product niche market, enhancing the conversion strategy, creating the selling material in order to form a sales magnet combo for customer adquisition.

We have gained over $500,000USD worth in revenue for startups, helped raised investment of over $450,000USD and made stock exit of +$100,000 and much more!


Why choose us?

The best thing you can do, is to hire talent and experience and where you can monitor all the sales process. For this we provide one to one customer support and realtime metrics on client prospection, meetings, sales funnel and client engagement.


You and your team are going to have account access to follow up at all times the CRM (Customer Relationship Management) status, growth & value.

Our Business

We understand how hard it is to startup a business while having a fulltime job. Having a minimum viable product while being fully commited to a great employer takes up too much risk to make the transition without having a previous market validation.

Prospecting the desired industry and companies is time consuming. It also requires specific skills in sales approach and business knowledge for the product to succeed in the market.

Competition is everywhere, so in order to stand out it is necessary an eagle eye for customer selection and superb sales mixin.

We often work with talented software engineers and marketing gurus, with unique minimum viable product ready for growth and scalability.

Our Methodology

We start by evaluating the actual status of the product related to the short and mid term desired sales goals.

  • Identify potential niche market and customer categories.
  • Analyze competition weaknesses and market product failures.
  • Highlight product key features.
  • Design and create the sales mix combo.
  • Execute strategy, generate meetings and closing deals.
  • Manage reports, analyze data, enhance strategy & repeat.
